Welcome

If you're reading this, it means you have stumbled across my page by the forces of the food God's...or you need a recipe for any occasion at any expertise level. Regularly, I sift through tons, and I do mean TONS of recipes, whether it's from recipe books, the food network (which I may or may not spend all of my free time watching), or the classic recipes that are passed down to me. I wasn't always the strongest in the kitchen, and for that reason, I never want anyone to get discouraged from beginning their journey simply because recipes are "too hard". So, I have condensed my favourite recipes here into three categories:

  1. Breakfast/Brunch
  2. Dinner
  3. Dessert

I have a recipe that caters to the different chef- or baker experience levels within each respective category. So on days, you want a more straightforward recipe, or if you are just getting started, "Home Chef". For recipes that are at an intermediate-advanced level, "Restaurant Style" is where it's at. And finally, to top it off with Michelin style restaurant recipes, "Chef Style" is undoubtedly to impress anyone, although, be ready to work for it.
