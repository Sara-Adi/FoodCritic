About Me

Sara's Kitchen Reviews

Sara’s kitchen is a popular media kitchen blogging production site that attempts to recreate cooking recipes that are accessible to people with background journeys: whether you dreamed of imitating world class chefs, redesign an amazing restaurant that you last visited with your family, or enjoy cooking homemade food with the comfort of implementing your own ingredients. An author to multiple New York Times best-selling cookbooks, such as Love Yourself and the Kitchen and Cheap Saffron: Become a World-Class chef at home for less.

The New York Times have described my cookbooks to be “simple, delicious, and affordable to get every beginner started on developing a long-lasting passion for cooking - all at comfort of your home too”. Whether it is grass-fed, meat-based or seasonal, plant-based ingredients, we have you fully covered to get your journey started.
